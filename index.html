Multiple modifier

You can use multiple modifier at the same time! It's dead simple – just seperate them with a space. 

<div class="grid" data-grid="center middle reverse">
	<div class="grid__item" data-grid="3/12">
		…
	</div>
	<div class="grid__item" data-grid="3/12">
		…
	</div>
	<div class="grid__item" data-grid="3/12">
		…
	</div>
</div>
